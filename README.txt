SaiCoPvP AutoFish

* /Mutefish - Mutes All Fishing Sounds. (Can be Toggle on/off)
* /tomm1k - Plays Voice Clips from Tomm1k on certain Events.
* Autofish (Default F10) - Works With/Without Sound.
* Inventory Checker - Always enabled, This calculates the value of your inventory when the mod is disabled, Shows you a breakdown and allows you to just Click it to /sell all.
